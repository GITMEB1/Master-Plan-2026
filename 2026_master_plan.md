## 2. "Zero-Fail" Automation Protocol (Inbox/Admin)

**The Problem**: Inbox avoidance leads to financial danger (IVA/Red letters).
**The Fix**: A system that separates "Noise" from "Nuclear".

### The "Traffic Light" Triage System
**Category RED (Nuclear)**
- **Keywords**: "IVA", "HMRC", "Council Tax", "Landlord", "Tenancy", "Overdue", "Creditfix" (or your provider).
- **Action**: These bypass the inbox and trigger a **LOUD** alert.
- **Automation**:
    - *Gmail Filter*: `From:(*@creditfix.co.uk OR *@hmrc.gov.uk) OR Subject:(Overdue OR Urgent OR Arrears)` -> Apply Label "RED NUCLEAR" + Always Mark Important.
    - *n8n (Optional High-Value)*: Watch Gmail Label "RED NUCLEAR" -> Send Telegram/Whatsapp Msg to you AND Partner: "ðŸš¨ ACTION REQUIRED: [Subject]".

**Category AMBER (Human)**
- **Keywords**: Personal names, "Interview", "Offer", "Update".
- **Action**: Check once daily (11 AM).
- **Automation**: Filter bulk newsletters out ruthlessly.

**Category GREEN (Noise)**
- **Action**: Bulk archive weekly.

### Deployment Timetable
- **Week 1 (The Purge)**:
    - Day 1: Search `is:unread`. Select all. Mark as read (Yes, all). If it was vital from 3 months ago, they will have chased.
    - Day 2: Set up the RED filters (stated above). Test with a dummy email.
    - Day 3: Unsubscribe from 10 lists.
- **Week 2 (The Habit)**:
    - 9:00 AM: Phone check. RED label only.
    - 2:00 PM: AMBER label check. Reply to humans.
    - **Ongoing**: Weekly "Inbox Bankruptcy" every Friday at 4 PM. Archive everything older than 7 days.
